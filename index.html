🎯 Sales Positioning

Against Dext
While Dext excels at receipt and document capture, Lightyear adds the full AP control layer — PO creation, 3-way matching, and automated price checking against agreed supplier rates. Dext captures the data; Lightyear ensures what's been captured is correct before you pay.
On price
Dext is cheaper to start. But if a supplier overcharges you once, or a fraudulent bank detail slips through, the cost of that mistake far exceeds the price difference. Lightyear's automated price checking and fraud verification pay for themselves.
On AI
Dext's new AI Assist is strong for categorisation. Lightyear's Smart Extract+ handles data extraction from handwritten and poor-quality documents — including supplier statements — and adds the controls Dext doesn't offer.
